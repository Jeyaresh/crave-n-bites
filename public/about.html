Our Story

About us

In the year 2024, a delicious dream came to life in Nagercoil — and that dream is Crave & Bites. What began as a small home-based cake venture by a passionate baker is now one of the most loved destinations for custom cakes and sweet treats in town. At Crave & Bites, we believe every celebration deserves something special — and nothing says special like a freshly baked cake made with love. From birthdays and weddings to simple everyday joys, our creations are crafted to make your moments even more memorable. With a focus on quality, creativity, and customer delight, we offer a wide range of mouthwatering cakes, cupcakes, brownies, and other baked goodies. Every item is prepared in a clean, well-equipped kitchen using high-quality ingredients — and yes, everything is baked with love! Whether you’re looking for an elegant anniversary cake, a fun birthday theme cake, or a quick box of brownies to treat yourself, Crave & Bites has something perfect for you. We’re also proud to be among the few bakeries in Nagercoil offering home delivery. Just give us a call at 9003 9003 74, and we’ll deliver your order right to your doorstep, fresh and fabulous. At Crave & Bites, we don’t just bake — we create joy, one bite at a time.
